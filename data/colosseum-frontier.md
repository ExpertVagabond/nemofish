# Colosseum Frontier Hackathon — April 2026

## Overview
Colosseum Frontier is Solana's premier hackathon with $250K pre-seed funding per winner (up to 15 winners) plus accelerator admission. Registration opens April 6, submissions close May 11.

## Tracks
1. **Consumer** — User-facing apps, social, gaming interfaces
2. **DeFi** — Decentralized finance, lending, DEXes, yield
3. **Stablecoins** — Stablecoin infrastructure, payments, on/off ramps
4. **DePIN** — Decentralized physical infrastructure (satellites, sensors, IoT)
5. **Gaming** — On-chain games, game infrastructure
6. **Infrastructure** — Developer tools, SDKs, node infrastructure, wallets
7. **Artificial Intelligence** — AI agents, ML on-chain, AI-powered tools
